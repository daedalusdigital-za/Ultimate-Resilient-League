01

Executive Summary

URL Coin (URL) is the official internal utility currency of the Ultimate Resilient League (URL), a competitive esports ecosystem designed to combine performance-based valuation, team ownership mechanics, structured transfers, and controlled competitive balance.

URL Coin is not designed as a speculative investment token. It exists strictly to power the league's internal economic system.

The purpose of URL Coin is to:

  • Standardize player valuation
  • Enable controlled team ownership mechanics
  • Power transfer markets
  • Distribute league rewards
  • Maintain competitive balance
  • Sustain the URL ecosystem
02

The Problem

Traditional esports leagues lack:

  • Transparent internal player valuation systems
  • Structured team asset growth
  • Balanced economic incentives
  • Controlled reward redistribution
  • Sustainable internal currency systems

Many gaming ecosystems suffer from:

  • Inflation
  • Overpowered roster stacking
  • Uncontrolled spending
  • No performance-based asset model

URL Coin solves these structural inefficiencies.

03

The Solution: The URL Ecosystem

URL introduces:

  • Fixed entry franchise structure (10 teams)
  • Performance-based team valuation
  • Controlled player market pricing
  • Salary cap model
  • Transfer windows
  • Revenue redistribution model

URL Coin serves as the economic backbone.

05

Tokenomics

5.1 Supply Model

URL Coin operates under a controlled issuance model. Supply is not infinite.

Coins enter circulation through:

  • Franchise buy-ins
  • Sponsorship allocation
  • League treasury allocation
  • Reward pools

Coins are removed from circulation via:

  • Transfer tax burns
  • Penalty deductions
  • League fee allocations

06

Player Valuation Model

120 Min Value (URL)
600 Max Value (URL)
20% Max of Lowest Franchise
Rating Formula

Rating = 50% Points + 30% Kills + 20% Average Kills

Converted to URL using capped scaling formula

This ensures star players remain valuable, no single player destabilizes team economics, and competitive balance remains intact.

07

Team Structure

Each team consists of:

1 Owner
1 Manager
6 Active Players

Owners: Purchase franchises, hold team asset rights, receive performance-based upside.

Managers: Control roster, execute transfers, manage salary cap.

Players: Earn value through performance, increase team valuation.

08

Competitive Balance System

To prevent dominance cycles:

  • Two transfer windows per season
  • Salary cap model
  • Luxury tax penalties
  • Relegation system (bottom 3 at risk)

Relegation resets franchise position and impacts valuation.

09

Revenue Model

Revenue sources include:

  • Franchise buy-ins
  • Sponsorship contributions
  • Controlled betting pool (if enabled)
  • League partnerships

Revenue distribution:

  • Performance rewards
  • Treasury reserve growth
  • Operational sustainability
  • Ecosystem reinvestment

Betting (if enabled) is capped and regulated internally to avoid systemic risk.

10

Stability Mechanisms

URL Coin includes:

  • Transfer tax (burn mechanism)
  • Controlled issuance
  • Salary cap
  • Valuation ceilings
  • Performance-weighted scaling
  • Treasury stabilization reserve

These mechanisms prevent inflation, hyper-concentration of power, economic collapse, and asset bubbles.

11

Governance

Governance may include:

  • League Council
  • Owner voting
  • Manager voting
  • Weighted voting by team performance

Future DAO implementation is optional and not required for early phases.

12

Roadmap

Internal league utility only. Controlled closed ecosystem.

Phase 2

Expanded marketplace. Advanced analytics.

Phase 3

Phase 4

Regional expansion.

13

Legal & Risk Disclaimer

URL Coin is:

  • An internal ecosystem utility
  • Not a publicly tradable security
  • Not an investment contract
  • Not a guaranteed return instrument

All economic participation carries risk. League governance reserves the right to adjust economic parameters to preserve ecosystem stability.

14

Core Philosophy

URL Coin is designed to:
Reward performance
Protect competitive balance
Sustain league growth
Prevent economic distortion
Maintain long-term viability

This is not speculation.
This is structured competitive economics.
