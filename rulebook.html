01

League Overview

The Ultimate Resilient League (URL) is a competitive esports league built around a franchise model where teams are owned and managed by individuals who recruit and manage players throughout the season.

The league combines competitive gameplay, team ownership, and an internal digital ecosystem powered by URL Coins, which act as the league's operational currency for transfers, bidding, and internal economic activity.

Goal: Create a fair, transparent, and competitive environment where players, managers, and team owners can compete at a high level while contributing to a sustainable esports ecosystem.

02

League Structure

The URL operates with 10 franchise teams competing over the course of a season.

10 Teams
6 Players/Team
4 Per Match
3 Matches/Day

Each team consists of:

  • 1 Team Owner
  • 1 Manager
  • 6 Registered Players

Only 4 players participate per match in Battle Royale matches.

Teams compete in 3 matches per day during scheduled matchdays.

Matches run Monday to Friday for the duration of the season.

The league standings are determined by total accumulated points.

Team Ownership

Franchise teams are purchased for:

R1,000 ZAR
5,000 URL Coins

Ownership grants the right to:

  • Recruit and trade players
  • Participate in the league ecosystem

Note: Team values may fluctuate based on performance within the league.

05

Player Registration

All players must register before the start of the season.

Required information includes:

  • In-game name (IGN)
  • Discord ID
  • Region
  • Availability

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT

Players MUST participate in pre-season lobbies to verify eligibility. Players who have not participated in these lobbies may not join teams mid-season.

Only registered players are eligible to participate in league matches.

No Exceptions: No one can take part just because they are mates – real money is involved and all players must adhere to the registration process.

06

Draft & Player Selection

Managers recruit players from the registered player pool.

If multiple managers want the same player, a bidding system using URL Coins is used.

Example:

Player starting bid: 200 URL

Managers may bid higher amounts until one manager wins the auction.

The highest bidder secures the player.

07

Transfer Windows

Transfers are only allowed during official transfer windows.

Transfer windows occur:

Weekend 1 of the season Weekend 2 of the season

IMPORTANT

Outside these windows, player transfers are NOT permitted.

08

Transfer Restrictions

To prevent exploitation of the transfer system:

  • Cooldown Period: Players who have been transferred cannot transfer again immediately
  • Review Process: Suspicious transfers may be reviewed and require approval from league administrators
  • No Mid-Week Signings: Emergency player replacements are NOT allowed mid-week

ROSTER PROTECTION RULE

If a player leaves the team during the week, the team must continue with the remaining players until the next transfer window. If you have 6 players and one drops out, you must manage with the remaining 5 – you cannot bring in an outsider mid-week or once transfers have closed.

Violations May Result In:

  • Transfer being nullified
  • URL Coin penalties
  • League point deductions
  • Suspension from future transfer windows
09

Match Attendance

Teams must attend all scheduled matches.

If a team does not appear for a match:

  • The team is placed 10th for that match
  • The team receives 1 placement point
  • Kill points = 0

Repeat Offenders

  • Additional point deductions
  • Potential franchise suspension
  • Forced forfeiture of remaining matches

  • Observers must remain neutral

Two-Step Verification: Match results will go through a two-step verification process before being officially recorded.

18

Transparency & Calculation Formulas

To ensure competitive integrity and transparency, all scoring, standings, and ecosystem values in the Ultimate Resilient League are calculated using publicly defined formulas.

All calculations are applied automatically by the league system and verified before publication.

18.1 Match Points Calculation

Each match awards points based on team placement and kills.

Placement Points

Placement Points

10th Place1

Kill Points

Each kill = 1 point

Match Points Formula:

Match Points = Placement Points + Total Kills

Example:

Team finishes 3rd with 18 kills

  • Placement Points = 8
  • Kill Points = 18

Match Points = 8 + 18 = 26

18.2 League Standings Calculation

League standings are determined by the total accumulated points across all matches.

League Points Formula:

League Points = Sum of all Match Points

Example:

  • Match 1 = 31 points
  • Match 2 = 26 points
  • Match 3 = 40 points

League Points = 31 + 26 + 40 = 97

Teams are ranked by highest league points.

18.3 Team Kill Statistics

Total kills are tracked for each team throughout the season.

Team Kill Formula:

Team Kills = Sum of all kills across matches

Example:

  • Match 1 = 26 kills
  • Match 2 = 4 kills
  • Match 3 = 30 kills

Total Kills = 60

Kills are used for statistics and franchise valuation calculations.

18.4 Franchise Value Calculation (URL Ecosystem)

Each team begins with a base franchise value.

5,000 Base URL
R1,000 ZAR Value

Conversion Rate: 1 URL Coin = 0.20 ZAR

18.5 Team Performance Index

Team value changes based on performance. The performance index combines:

  • League Points
  • Total Kills
  • League Ranking

Normalization Variables

Pmax = highest points in league

Kmax = highest kills in league

Performance Ratios

Points Ratio = Team Points / Pmax

Kills Ratio = Team Kills / Kmax

18.6 Performance Index Formula

Performance Index =

(0.6 × Points Ratio) +

(0.3 × Kills Ratio) +

(0.1 × Rank Bonus)

Rank Bonus Values

Rank Bonus
1st1.0
2nd0.9
3rd0.8
4th0.7
5th0.6
6th0.5
7th0.4
8th0.3
9th0.2
10th0.1

18.7 Team Value Multiplier

The performance index is converted into a multiplier.

Multiplier = 0.7 + (Performance Index × 1.3)

This creates a value range between:

0.7× Minimum
2.0× Maximum

18.8 Franchise Value Formula

Franchise Value = Base Value × Multiplier

Example:

  • Base Value = 5,000 URL
  • Multiplier = 1.75

Franchise Value = 8,750 URL

18.9 Player Rating Calculation

Players are evaluated based on their performance. The rating considers:

  • Total kills
  • Match appearances
  • Average kills per match

Average Kills = Total Kills / Matches Played

Example:

  • Kills = 53
  • Matches = 9

Average = 5.89 kills per match

Players are assigned a rating score (0–99) based on performance relative to other players. Player value is then derived from rating.

18.10 No-Show Penalty

If a team does not attend a match:

Automatic Penalties Applied:

  • Placement = 10th
  • Placement Points = 1
  • Kills = 0
  • Match Points = 1

Repeated no-shows may result in additional penalties.

18.11 Verification Process

All calculations are verified using a two-step system.

1 Admin calculates results
2 Independent verifier confirms calculations
3 Results published

This system ensures transparency and prevents disputes.
